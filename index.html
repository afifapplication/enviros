About Us

Envirosh Engineering & Consultancy is a Malaysian-based company established on 27th December 2018 under the Company Act 1965. Operating as a sole proprietorship, we specialize in providing integrated Environmental, Safety & Health (ESH) solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of businesses across various industries.

As a trusted name in inspection, certification, testing, and verification services, we help organizations navigate and comply with local, national, and international ESH regulations. Our mission is to empower businesses to adopt best practices that safeguard their workforce, the environment, and their operational integrity.

With a multidisciplinary team combining expertise in Engineering, Occupational Safety & Health (OSH), General Management, and Medical OSH, we offer practical, effective solutions. Whether you're looking to enhance compliance, manage risks, or create a safer workplace, Envirosh delivers strategies rooted in industry-leading knowledge and real-world experience.


We ensure your operations comply fully with key ESH Acts:

  • Occupational Safety and Health Act 1994 and Regulations (OSHA)
  • Factory and Machinery Act 1967 and Regulations (FMA)
  • Petroleum (Safety Measures) Act 1984 (PSMA)
  • Environmental Quality Act 1974 (EQA)

For the environment: Our specialists guide all environmental responsibilities, offering solutions:

  • Air: Ambient and indoor air sampling and testing services
  • Soil: Drilling, sampling, testing, and geotechnical support provided
  • Water: Comprehensive sampling and analysis of all water types
  • Waste: Waste assessment, testing, monitoring, and reduction strategies available
  • Noise: Accurate noise level measurement and environmental compliance support
